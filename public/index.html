Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Sleep Well?

Sleep Well is a sleep and wellness tracking app built for firefighters. It helps users track sleep, fatigue, stress, and daily habits to better understand recovery and long-term health.

2. Who is Sleep Well for?

Sleep Well is designed primarily for firefighters (career and volunteer) who work long or rotating shifts.

3. Does Sleep Well provide medical advice?

No. Sleep Well is for informational and wellness purposes only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional with questions about your health.

4. I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at mackenziekettwig@gmail.com with a description of the issue, screenshots if possible, and information about your device and iOS version.

5. How do I delete my data or request my data?

If you would like to request deletion of your data or ask for a copy of your data, please contact us at mackenziekettwig@gmail.com and include the email address associated with your account. We will follow up with next steps.
